## Why Color Highlighter?

Reading `#3498db` or `rgb(52, 152, 219)` tells you very little at a glance.
Color Highlighter renders the actual color directly in your editor so you can
_see_ what you are coding.

It works out of the box, supports a huge range of languages and formats, and is
fully configurable so you only see the highlights you want.

Head over to the [Getting Started](/guide/getting-started) guide to install it,
or browse the [Features](/guide/features) to see everything it can do.
